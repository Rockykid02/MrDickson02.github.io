    What is Hacking

    Gaining Access to a system that are not supposed to have access to is considered as Hacking,For example reading information that you are not supposed to be able to read or gaining access to a computer or phone that you are not supposed to have access

    What is Ethical Hacking

    Ethical hacking is the process of using hacking skills for positive purposes, such as testing the security of computer systems and networks, or finding and fixing vulnerabilities before they can be exploited by malicious hackers. Ethical hackers, also known as "white hat hackers," work with organizations to help them improve their security. They use their skills to find and fix security holes, without causing any harm or damage. Ethical hacking is an important part of keeping computer systems and networks safe from Attacks

    Types of Hacking

    we can define hacking in different categories depending on what is being hacked..These are as follows

    • WhatsApp Hacking
    • Computer Hacking
    • Password Hacking
    • Email Hacking

    WhatsApp Hacking

    WhatsApp hacking is the process of gaining unauthorized access to a WhatsApp account, typically through the use of malware or other hacking techniques. WhatsApp hackers may try to gain access to WhatsApp messages, contacts, or even call logs. This type of hacking can be dangerous, as it can lead to the theft of personal information or even financial fraud. WhatsApp users should be aware of the risks of hacking and take steps to protect their accounts, such as using strong passwords and two-factor authentication

    Computer Hacking

    Computer hacking is the process of gaining unauthorized access to a computer system or network, in order to steal data, cause damage, or gain control of the system. Computer hackers use a variety of techniques to break into systems, including social engineering, brute force attacks, and exploiting vulnerabilities in software or hardware

    Password Hacking

    Password hacking, also known as password cracking, is the process of guessing or cracking a password in order to gain unauthorized access to a system or account. Password hackers may use techniques such as brute force attacks, dictionary attacks, or rainbow table attacks to guess or crack a password.

    Email Hacking

    Email Hacking means unauthorized access to an Email account and using it without owners permission

    Types Of Hackers

    • Black Hat Hackers
    • White Hat Hackers
    • Grey Hat Hackers

      • Black Hat Hackers

      Black hat hackers are hackers who use their skills for malicious purposes, such as stealing data, damaging systems, or making a profit. They are in contrast to white hat hackers, who use their skills for ethical purposes. Black hat hackers may use a variety of techniques to break into systems, including social engineering, malware, and exploiting vulnerabilities. They may also sell the data they steal on the dark web. Black hat hacking is a serious crime that can have serious consequences.

      NB Black Hat Hacking is illegal

      White Hat Hackers

      White hat hackers, also known as ethical hackers, are hackers who use their skills for positive or ethical purposes. They may be hired by companies to test their systems for vulnerabilities, or they may hack systems as a way to expose security flaws so that they can be fixed. White hat hackers operate within the law, and with the permission of the system owners. They often use the same techniques as black hat hackers, but with the goal of improving security rather than causing harm

      Grey Hat Hackers

      These are Hybrib between Black and White Hackers ..Grey hat hackers, sometimes called "hacktivists," They may hack systems without permission, but they typically don't have malicious intent. Instead, they may hack a system to expose a vulnerability, or to make a political statement. They may also hack for fun or for personal gain, but without causing any damage.
